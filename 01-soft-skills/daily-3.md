# The Daily 3

## What it is
The Daily 3 is a simple routine for improving focus, clarity, and well-being.

## The 3 Activities

### 1. Movement
At least 30 minutes of physical activity every day.

### 2. Meditation
Taking time to calm the mind and improve focus.

### 3. Writing (Morning Reflection)
Writing down thoughts in the morning to clear the mind and set intentions.

## Why it matters
- Improves mental clarity
- Boosts productivity
- Helps manage stress

## My experience
I am learning to apply this daily to stay consistent and focused.
