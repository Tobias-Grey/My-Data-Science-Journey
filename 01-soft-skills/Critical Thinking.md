# Critical Thinking

## What I learned
Critical thinking helps me analyze problems and make better decisions.

## USE
I used it when breaking down tasks during team work.

# Convergent vs Divergent Thinking

## Divergent Thinking
Divergent thinking is about generating many ideas.
It is creative and open-ended.

## Convergent Thinking
Convergent thinking is about narrowing down ideas to the best solution.
It is logical and focused.

## When To Use Them
- Divergent: brainstorming ideas in a team
- Convergent: selecting the best solution to a problem
